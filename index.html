About Us

Who Are We?

ALLTECHBIZ CONCEPTS & VISION LTD. (AllTechBiZ) is a cutting edge, full service information tehnology consulting firm, which was created to help Small Medium Enterprises synergically improve the effectiveness of using Information Technology - especially software solutions - to meet their strategic business objectives.

We specialize in providing Microsoft-based Financial and Business Management Solutions such as accounting and finance, project accounting, financial reporting and enterprise resource planning (ERP) solutions. Our approach is a differentiated and focused one that bridges gaps and enables smooth adaption of technological solutions among business stakeholders. Since our incorporation on June 7th, 2013 as a registered, privately held, family owned and profitable limited liabilty company, we've advanced to becoming one of the top Microsoft Dynamics SL Partners of the region and a Microsoft Silver Enterprise Resource Planning, Value Added Solution Partner. We maintain our tax compliancy and are duly registered and approved by the National Contracts Commission (NCC).
